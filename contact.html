Publish An Article for Connect 2 Change

Why It Is Important:

Student journalism is a great extracurricular and is so beneficial to our peers. Connect 2 Change does take pride in smart and capable teenagers writing important subjects that are prevalent in our world today. You do NOT have to be some sort of an extraordinary writer or a famous activist. As long as you show passion in your writing and the issue being written, that's all we can ask!

What We Ask Of You:

Write a detailed, honest article about a topic, event, issue of your choosing and then choose to submit it. Then, an editor will be in touch regarding final editing touches(grammar, making sure the correct information is given etc.), layout of article with images chosen and the date of publishing!

You Must Be/Have:

  • In grade 7-12
  • An interest in writing and journalism
  • Adequate writing skills
  • Access to internet and technology to respond to emails quickly
  • Ability to devote time in creating quality work
